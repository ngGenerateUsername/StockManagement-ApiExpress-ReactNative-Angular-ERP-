HITS of Bizerte

The Higher Institute of Technological Studies of Bizerte was created by Decree n °: 2005 - 1458 of May 5, 2005. It comes under the general direction of technological studies of the Ministry of higher education and scientific research
    ISET de Bizerte is a public scientific and technological establishment. Its main mission is to contribute to socio-economic development and technology transfer in the Bizerte region as well as to strengthen the employability and professional integration of higher education graduates.
